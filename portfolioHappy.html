Happyculture - a game for a botanic garden

The application Happyculture is a team project carried out with an agile methodology. The objective was to make for a client (a botanical garden) a game for their visitors. The application was to allow them to discover the world of bees and flowers. It was made by 4 people and we used methodological tools allowing it to be organised in the same way as Trello.
